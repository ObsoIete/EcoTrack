🌱 EcoTrack: Project Documentation

Project Description

EcoTrack is an intuitive web-based dashboard designed to help individuals visualize the environmental impact of their daily commute. By calculating carbon emissions (CO₂e) based on distance and transportation mode, the app transforms abstract data into actionable insights, providing personalized "Green Tips" and an annual tree-offset estimate to encourage more sustainable travel habits.

How to Use Instructions
  • Input Distance: Enter your total daily commute distance in miles in the provided field.
  • Select Transport: Choose your mode of travel—either by clicking the visual icon badges or using the dropdown menu.
  • Analyze: Click the "Calculate" button to reveal your daily impact and your yearly tree-offset score.
  • Improve: Read the personalized "Green Tip" for suggestions on how to lower your carbon footprint.
