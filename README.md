# 🌱 EcoTrack: Carbon Footprint Calculator

### **Project Description**
EcoTrack is an intuitive web-based dashboard designed to help individuals visualize the environmental impact of their daily commute. By calculating carbon emissions (CO₂e) based on distance and transportation mode, the app provides actionable insights, including personalized "Green Tips" and an annual tree-offset estimate.

### **How to Use**
1. **Input Distance:** Enter your total daily commute distance in miles.
2. **Select Transport:** Choose your mode of travel (Gas Car, EV, Bus, or Bicycle).
3. **Analyze:** Click "Calculate" to reveal your impact and tree-offset score.
4. **Improve:** Read the personalized tip to lower your footprint.
